ABOUT ME


I'm Defne Deda, a creative technologist and designer with a deep passion for blending technology and art. With a background in Computer Science and Psychology from Northwestern University, I thrive at the intersection of design, music, and entertainment. My experience spans branding, event production, and user-centric design, including leading one of the largest student-run music festivals in the country and collaborating with artists and brands to create unforgettable visual experiences. I bring a unique mix of strategic thinking, hands-on design, and a relentless drive to push creative boundaries.
