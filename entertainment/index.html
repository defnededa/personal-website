Entertainment and Promotions:

Mayfest Productions

Mayfest Productions plans and produces the nation's largest student-run music festival — Dillo Day, with over 12,000 festival attendees, set on the Northwestern Campus. In addition to the festival, Mayfest hosts and promotes a number of other programming events including Battle of the Bands and Battle of the DJs. Learn more here.

Below you can see my path chronologically, year by year.

2025: Theme TBD, Mayfest Productions Co-chair

Main Responsibilities:
Leading the executive board of 18 members and managing a $500,000 budget for the largest student-run music festival in the country, with over 12,000 attendees.
Overseeing festival operations, including two stages featuring 15 artists performing over 9.5 hours.
Partnering with Northwestern University administration and the City of Evanston to implement safety and community relations initiatives to ensure a secure and successful event.

2024: Camp Dillo, Promotions Co-Director

While I am one of the co-chairs of the entire organization now, my background is in Promotions. The Promotions committee designs and distributes all promotional materials for Mayfest Productions and Dillo Day. They handle artist announcements, merchandise, digital content, and marketing campaigns, engaging the student body through social media to manage the organization's brand and keep students updated.

Main Responsibilities:
Leading a team of 15 in developing branding strategies and managing partnerships.
Communicating with talent agencies to get marketing approval for promotional materials.
Overseeing artist announcements, sponsorship posts, and lineup posters over multiple platforms to ensure they both maintained brand consistency and adhered to contracts with our artists and sponsors, such as Nyx, Bumble, and more.

2023: Planet Dillo, Lead Brand Designer
