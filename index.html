Defne Deda

As a junior at Northwestern University, I am pursuing a major in Computer Science with a minor in Psychology, complemented by a Segal Design Certificate. My particular fascination lies in the intricate dynamics of human interaction with technology, and its implications for designers, programmers, and users alike. My aspiration is to craft impactful experiences and conceive user-centric solutions that are not only ingrained in social familiarity but also stir excitement.
